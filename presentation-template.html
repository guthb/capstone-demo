Project Goals:

  • Provide multiple users a place where they can create a record of their vehicle(s)and store for retrieval from a virtual garage.
  • Allow the entry of service items, fuel, oil, maintenance and tires.
  • Allow the review of all service entries for each vehicle in their own log.
  • Allow the search of any open recalls on the vehicles.

Demo

Time for a live demo!

Lessons Learned:

  • Be realistic with your time required for the functionality you are building.
  • Test your API calls first.
  • Keep your data structures as flat as possible.
  • Take your time with Materalize.
